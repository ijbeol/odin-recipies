Description

The pizza is an italian dish that is easy to make and tastes well. It is based on flour and water dough, usually topped with meat and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Make the pizza dough
  2. Put the tomato sauce on the pizza dough
  3. Put pepperonies all around
  4. Sprinkle cheese on top
  5. Heat in oven for 8 minutes at 250 degrees celsius
