Description

The lasagna is an italian dish that is easy to make and tastes well. It is based on pasta plates, usually filled with a meat and cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta plates
  • Meat sauce
  • Cheese sauce

Steps

  1. Put pasta plates at the bottom
  2. Make the meat sauce
  3. Put the meat sauce on top of the pasta plates
  4. Put the cheese sauce on top of the meat sauce
  5. Repeat step 1 to 4 three times
  6. Sprinkle cheese on top
  7. Heat in oven for 45 minutes at 200 degrees celsius
