Description

The pasta is an italian dish that is easy to make and tastes well. It is based on pasta, usually complemented by a ragu.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Make the meat sauce
  3. Rinse and put the pasta on a plate
  4. Put the meat sauce on top of the pasta
  5. Shred parmesan on top
